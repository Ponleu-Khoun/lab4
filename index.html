Brief History of Cambodia

Planning a trip to Cambodia can be an exciting adventure! Cambodia is a country with a rich history, stunning natural beauty, and a unique culture. From the magnificent Angkor Wat temples to the pristine beaches and lush jungles, Cambodia has a lot to offer to travelers.Cambodia has a rich history dating back to the Khmer Empire, which thrived from the 9th to the 15th century. The empire's capital, Angkor, was known for its grand temples, including Angkor Wat which was built in the 12th century. In the 19th century, Cambodia became a French colony. After gaining independence in 1953, Cambodia went through a turbulent period, including the Khmer Rouge regime led by Pol Pot in the 1970s, resulting in a tragic genocide.
