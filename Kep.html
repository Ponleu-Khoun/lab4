what is about Keb?

Kep city is Southern of Cambodia, it borders Kampot to the North, West and East; and it is the neighborhood of the Gulf of Thailand in the South. The city is of close proximity to Kampot, which is a more popular tourism spot than Kep. From Kampot, it takes roughly 30 to 45 minutes to drive, so the city is often visited by people in one-day trip. This town wraps inside a long history of Cambodia because it used to be the resort town of the French and Cambodian high-flyers until the late 1970s. Nowadays, Kep is actually most preferred by local people. However, it starts to welcome more and more foreigners coming for a few days in order to unwind their body and mind.
